Since 2014, Letsfly.lk is a fast-growing company in the travel industry in Sri Lanka (world’s top tourist destination) and a business to business provider for travel services to wholesalers, tour operators as well as corporate companies. Letsfly.lk is a Sri Lankan incoming travel agency, specialized in the organization of tailor-made high-quality tours all over Sri Lanka. We offer a large variety of tours which show the very best that Sri Lanka has to offer in terms of culture, heritage, nature adventure and beach. Whether you are an established group or individual travelers, just tell us what you want to do, where/ when you want to go and Letsfly.lk takes care of the rest. We hope you enjoy our products and services as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
