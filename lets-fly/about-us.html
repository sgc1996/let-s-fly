Who We Are

We are specialized in providing total Travel Solutions to both Corporate and Leisure markets. Whilst being strategically located in Heart of Colombo, we provide 24 HOURS, 365 DAYS services. Passengers can call anytime to obtain flight information, purchase air tickets, book hotels or even discuss their future holiday travel plans.
