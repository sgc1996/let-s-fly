Honeymoon Tour

Begin your new journey together with an unforgettable experience in the gorgeous island of Sri Lanka. Take a trip to a host of beautiful locations within this colorful haven as you explore ancient ruins, take romantic strolls in the hills, go in search of exotic wild creatures and bathe in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean. Enjoy an exclusive package which will give you a complete look into the fascinating landscape of Sri Lanka and leave you with the most magical honeymoon experience you could ever dream of. Visit the UNESCO world heritage sites, cultural cities, walk in village and experience the village life, Wildlife safari in national park and explore wild life, trek in national park, cooking class and learn varies types of spices how it makes tasty. You will visit the UNESCO-listed cities of Sigiriya, Polonnaruwa and Kandy with stops at Elephant Orphanage, Spice Garden, Temple of the Tooth and more. You will visit another UNESCO Heritage site in Galle, the Galle fort and this tour included the Whale watching in Mirissa and boat ride on Maadu river.

DAY 01    AIRPORT TO NEGOMBO (Excluding sightseeing)
Overnight stay :
Heritance Negombo Hotel  
Airport Garden Hotel  
Pledge 3 Hotel  
  • Arrival to Sri Lanka and you will be met a Letsfly.lk representative on arrival at the airport and transfer to Negombo. After checking-in to hotel you will be taken on a City tour.
DAY 02 - 03    NEGOMBO TO DAMBULLA (Excluding sightseeing)
Overnight stay :
Heritance Kandalama Hotel  
Camellia Resort & Spa Hotel  
Elephas Resort  
  • Proceed to Dambulla. En-route, you will visit Elephant Orphanage and your first attraction in Dambulla, the Rock Cave Temple. Next day morning you will travel to Sigiriya & Polonnaruwa. You will have an experience of village walk with a villager in Habarana and Minneriya Safari.
DAY 04    DAMBULLA TO KANDY (Excluding sightseeing)
Overnight stay :
The Golden Crown Hotel  
Madulkelle Eco Lodge Hotel  
Ozo Kandy Hotel  
  • After breakfast proceed to Kandy. En route you will visit Spice Garden in Matale and Batik factory. You will be taken on a city tour in Kandy. You will visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, Gem museum, Kandyan art & craft center. you will head to a local hall to watch a show of traditional Sri Lankan music, dance and drumming.
DAY 05    KANDY TO BENTOTA (Excluding sightseeing)
Overnight stay :
The Eden Resort  
Avani Bentota Hotel  
Yathra Houseboat by Jetwing Hotel  
  • After Breakfast proceed to Bentota. You will be taken to visit Galle fort, Turtle Hatchery, Boat ride on Maadu river, whale watching in Mirissa and the rest of the time you are free to spend as you wish.
DAY 06    BENTOTA TO AIRPORT
  • After breakfast direct transfer to the Airport for the departure flight.
