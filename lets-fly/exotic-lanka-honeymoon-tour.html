Exotic Lanka Honeymoon Tour

Begin your new journey together with an unforgettable experience in the gorgeous island of Sri Lanka. Take a trip to a host of beautiful locations within this colorful haven as you explore ancient ruins, take romantic strolls in the hills, go in search of exotic wild creatures and bathe in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean. Enjoy an exclusive package which will give you a complete look into the fascinating landscape of Sri Lanka and leave you with the most magical honeymoon experience you could ever dream of. Visit the UNESCO world heritage sites, cultural cities, walk in village and experience the village life, Wildlife safari in national park and explore wild life, trek in national park, cooking class and learn varies types of spices how it makes tasty. You will visit the UNESCO-listed cities of Sigiriya, Polonnaruwa and Kandy with stops at Elephant Orphanage, Spice Garden, Temple of the Tooth and more. You will visit another UNESCO Heritage site in Galle, the Galle fort and this tour included the Whale watching in Mirissa and boat ride on Maadu river.

GENERAL CANCELLATION POLICY

If cancelled 7 days prior to your arrival – No cancellation charged.

If cancelled less than 7 days prior to your arrival – 20% cancellation charged.

No show without informing - 100% cancellation charged.

VISA

A visa needs to be obtained online (http://www.eta.gov.lk) before traveling to Sri Lanka at a cost of $35 USD. Visa on arrival will not be allowed.

HOTELS, VISITS & GUEST MOVEMENT

Afterward guests will be able to use all hotel facilities and beach if available but will not be allowed to leave the hotel unless for visits in the itinerary above.

All excursions and visits will need to be approved by Sri Lanka Tourism if not allowed we will provide an alternative or may need to cancel. Allowed Excursions- https://www.srilanka.travel/helloagain/.

Timings and days of visits will vary depending on guidelines.

GENERAL NOTES

All drive times are approximate and are subject to change.

All reservations subject to availability at time of booking and Sri Lanka Health & Govt restrictions.

The standard check in time in Sri Lanka is 1400 hours and the Checkout time is 11am.

On Poya and other religious days alcohol will not be served at hotels and restaurants.

Please note that plucking of Tea leaves is not done on public holidays & Sundays. As such the Tea production process does not take place, however a tour of the factory will be arranged where the process is explained.

You can always change currency at the airport and a local SIM card can be purchased at the airport of Sri Lanka.

When visiting religious sites such as Kandy Temple attire should cover up to ankle length and sleeveless tops/shirts/t-shirts will not be allowed. It is also advised to bring a pair of socks as you will be asked to remove slippers/shoes when entering sacred temples.

If provincial or government taxes are adjusted or amended within the specified contract period, the local agent reserves the right to adjust the contracted rate accordingly to reflect the change.
